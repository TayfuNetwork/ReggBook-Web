1. Consent for Marketing Communications

By registering for ReggBook, I expressly consent to the processing of my contact information, such as email address and phone number, for the purpose of sending me commercial electronic communications about campaigns, discounts, new features, training, webinars and similar ReggBook services, within legal limits.

2. AI Hair Style Recommendation and Camera Use

The AI hair style recommendation feature in ReggBook is started only by the user or authorized business user. It processes camera frames temporarily on the device only to generate hair style recommendations.

  • No photo is taken or stored.
  • No video is recorded.
  • Camera images are not sent to ReggBook servers or Firebase.
  • Camera images are not shared with third parties.
  • No face image, face map, biometric template or permanent face data is created.
  • Face measurements, pixel values or face type results are not stored in the database.
  • Temporary camera and analysis data are discarded after the process ends.

This feature is not used for biometric authentication, identification or recognizing the user. If a hair style is selected, only the selected style file name or image reference may be associated with the customer record for future recommendation or reminder purposes. This is not face data.

3. Withdrawal of Consent

I understand that I may withdraw marketing communication consent at any time through app settings or by emailing info@reggbook.com.

If I do not want to use the AI hair style recommendation feature, I may refuse camera permission or disable it from device settings. Without camera permission, this feature will not work, while other basic appointment and customer management features may continue to be used.
